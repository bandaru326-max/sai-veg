Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ordering fresh Indian vegetables.

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery is absolutely FREE for orders of ₹199 or more. For smaller orders below ₹199, we charge a nominal delivery fee of ₹30 to cover shipping costs.

How do you guarantee vegetable freshness?

We procure vegetables at 04:00 AM daily from local mandis and cooperative farming blocks. They are cleaned, quality-sorted, packed in breathable bags, and delivered within hours of procurement.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order up to 1 hour before your selected delivery time slot begins. Simply call our support helpline with your Order ID.

Do you have a return/refund policy?

We follow a "No Questions Asked Return Policy". If you are not satisfied with the quality of any vegetable at the time of delivery, you can return it to the delivery agent. We will refund the amount instantly.
